Nature's Bounty & Wildlife Services' goals and objectives have evolved significantly since its establishment. At first the program focused on sustaining wildlife by preserving the environment. Now, the goal is still preserving nature, but also encouraging and teaching humans how to. The mission of Nature's Bounty & Wildlife Services is to address the wildlife's problems. The program is committed to the principle that wildlife is undomesticated animal species and we, the people, are responsible for protection of wildlife.
